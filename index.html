We founded BlackCarbon Recruitment out frustration

As a contracting software developer in the finance industry our founder found himself in a position where he needed to deal with recruitment agencies on a month to month basis.

This experience of agencies left a him feeling there is a lot of service potential missing in the industry.

This lead to the formation of BlackCarbon Recruitment with the ethos that is to bring value to everything we do.

But what does this actually mean?

For us this means that every interaction that we have with you we will be seeking to give you more value, whether this means giving you more information then you asked for or going the extra mile to filter candidate CV’s this is what we strive to do.
